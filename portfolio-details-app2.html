HealthQuest – Gamified Health Tracker using React Native

HealthQuest is a React Native mobile app that promotes healthier lifestyles through gamification and community support. It integrates daily challenges, food and water tracking, and exercise monitoring, rewarding users with coins, EXP, and achievements.

Features

  • Authentication Module:
    • Splash Screen – Initial branding/loading screen.
    • Login / Signup / Forgot Password – Firebase-powered authentication for secure account creation and recovery.
  • Home Module:
    • Dashboard Overview – Displays daily health challenges and quick access to tasks.
    • Quick stats for water intake, exercise, and nutrition.
  • Plan Module:
    • Meal Logging – Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner input with confirmation screens.
    • Personalized Food Plans – Users can track calorie intake and healthy eating habits.
  • Tasks Module:
    • Water Tracker – Track daily water intake (progress toward 8 glasses).
    • Exercise Tracker – Monitor workout activities and duration.
    • Eat Fruits & Vegetables Modal – Encourages healthier dietary practices.
  • Gamification Module:
    • Coins & EXP – Earned through completing tasks.
    • Achievements & Awards – Unlock badges (e.g., Nutrition Ninja, Hydro Hustler, Strength Sentinel, Fitness Trailblazer).
    • Leaderboards – Compare progress with other users.
  • Store Module:
    • Virtual Store – Spend coins on avatar customizations and digital items.
    • Item Modals – Preview and equip purchased items.
  • Settings Module:
    • Account Settings – Manage profile, password, and security.
    • Data Management – Integrated with Firebase Firestore and Storage.
