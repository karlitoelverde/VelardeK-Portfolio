ASLingo Mobile App using YOLOv8 Algorithm

ASLingo is an Android-based mobile learning application designed to bridge the communication gap between the Deaf and hearing communities through American Sign Language (ASL) education and real-time gesture detection.

Features

  • Learning Modules:
    • Structured into 3 proficiency levels – Basic, Intermediate, and Advanced.
    • Covers alphabets, numbers, greetings, WH-questions, colors, verbs, clothing, family, transportation, and more.
    • Animated 3D hand models for visual and kinesthetic learning.
  • Assessment Module:
    • Quizzes and multiple-choice questions to reinforce learning.
    • Tracks progress and provides immediate feedback.
  • Translation Module:
    • Sign-to-Text: Uses the device camera to recognize hand gestures in real time, converting them into text with text-to-speech output.
    • Text-to-Sign: Converts typed words into animated ASL gestures for interactive communication.
  • Real-Time Gesture Detection:
    • Powered by YOLOv8 + TensorFlow Lite.
    • Trained across 17 specialized models (alphabets, numbers, greetings, etc.) with an average accuracy of 96%.
    • Optimized for mobile deployment with dynamic model loading for performance.
