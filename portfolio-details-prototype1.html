PlanVista – Navigation App

PlanVista is a mobile navigation prototype designed to simplify route planning and enhance travel efficiency. The app features a clean, user-friendly interface with intuitive search, smart route suggestions, real-time navigation, and personalized saved locations. With smooth transitions and a consistent design language, PlanVista delivers an accessible and engaging navigation experience.

Features

  • Splash Screen:
    • Displays the PlanVista logo with brand colors, setting a modern and clean tone.
  • Login / Sign Up:
    • Simple form for account creation or sign-in.
    • Options for email, password, and social login.
  • Home / Dashboard:
    • Central hub with a destination search bar.
    • Quick access to Explore Nearby.
    • Bottom navigation for Home, Friends Section, Notification, and Settings.
  • Transitions & Interactions:
    • Smooth navigation between screens.
    • Interactive buttons and consistent design for intuitive use.
